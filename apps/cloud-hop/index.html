Cloud Hop

Cloud Hop is a simple but challenging puzzle platformer with a twist! You don't control the player, you control the platforms.

Drag and drop your blocks in the correct order to complete each level and hop your way to victory! Each block has a different effect or combination of effects and you will have to figure out all block interactions to master the game.

Cloud Hop is a solo developed game, made purely for the joy of game development. I hope you enjoy playing it as much as I did making it.
